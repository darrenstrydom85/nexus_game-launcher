Features

Everything you need to manage your library

Built for gamers who own titles across multiple stores and want a single, fast, beautiful hub.

Unified Library

Import games from Steam, Epic, GOG, Battle.net, and Amazon Games automatically. Add repacks and standalone games without a launcher too. One library, zero duplicates.

Play Time Tracking

Accurate session tracking across every source. See daily, weekly, and all-time stats at a glance.

Instant Launch

Click a game, it launches. Nexus handles the right storefront silently in the background.

Rich Metadata

Cover art, descriptions, genres, and ratings pulled from IGDB. Your library looks stunning by default.

Collections & Filters

Organise games into custom collections. Filter by status, genre, source, or play time.

Lightweight & Fast

Built with Tauri and Rust. Under 10 MB installed, minimal RAM usage, and native Windows performance.
