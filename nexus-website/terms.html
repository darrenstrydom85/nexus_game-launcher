02 / Service

Description of Service

Nexus is a free desktop application for Windows that unifies your PC game libraries from multiple launchers (Steam, Epic Games, GOG, Battle.net, Amazon Games) into a single interface. It provides play time tracking, library organisation, collections, and integrations with third-party services.
